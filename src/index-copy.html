HPSO

Healthcare Providers Service Organization (HPSO), through research and communication with APTA policyholders, is able to respond to the physical therapy community by offering insurance products tailored to the needs of physical therapists with quality coverage at affordable rates. In addition to the professional liability limits, coverage includes legal expense, license protection, deposition representation, defendant expense benefit, and much more. With over 112,000 physical therapists insured HPSO is the nation's No. 1 provider of professional liability insurance to PTs and PT practices.

RockTape

RockTape, established in 2009, is more than just a tape company; it is a movement company with a focus on cutting-edge education. Its flagship product, RockTape kinesiology tape, is used by health professionals, as well as consumers, to help with common sports and non-sports injuries. The tape is designed to relieve discomfort and enhance movement awareness. RockTape also offers a Functional Movement Training (FMT) certification. Courses are taught by industry-leading experts in movement assessment, performance, and rehabilitation. FMT courses present a revolutionary way of thinking about how we move and how we are injured. They include innovative movement-based mobility and stability tactics, practical kinesiology taping, IASTM, myofascial cupping, compression band flossing, and movement assessment.
